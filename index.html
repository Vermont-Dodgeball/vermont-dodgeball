About Vermont Dodgeball

Remember playing dodgeball in elementary school? We don't either but this is probably like that only you get to throw balls at your adult friends and other great people. We use rules based off of the American Dodgeball Association guidelines.

Vermont Dodgeball is a pickup dodgeball league. This means that you can just show up and play without a team. The teams are random and put together before we play. It's a fun way to relieve your Monday workday stress. It's only $5.00 per game and it's an awesome workout. Prepare to be sore.
